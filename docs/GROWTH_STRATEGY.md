## Revenue Paths

### 1. Enterprise Support (Easiest)
- **Model**: Paid SLA, priority support
- **Price**: $500-2000/month
- **Target**: Companies using agentic-brain in production
- **Differentiator**: On-prem deployment, compliance help

### 2. Hosted SaaS Version
- **Model**: Usage-based pricing
- **Price**: $0.01 per agent run, or $99/month unlimited
- **Target**: Teams who don't want to self-host
- **Stack**: Kubernetes, multi-tenant isolation (already built!)

### 3. Consulting & Implementation
- **Model**: Hourly/project-based
- **Price**: $150-300/hour
- **Target**: Enterprises building AI solutions
- **Scope**: Architecture, integration, training

### 4. Training & Workshops
- **Model**: Per-seat or corporate license
- **Price**: $500/person or $5000/team
- **Target**: AI teams, bootcamps
- **Format**: Live or recorded

### 5. Custom Features
- **Model**: Sponsored development
- **Price**: $5000-50000 per feature
- **Target**: Enterprises with specific needs
- **Example**: Custom vector DB adapter, compliance module
